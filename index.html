OUR STORY

The astoryTextroma of freshly baked bread, the sound of hot coffee pouring, enveloped by the faint scent of freshly cut grass in Hampstead Heath, and suddenly life takes a brief pause so that you can celebrate life itself. As the well-known resident poet of Hampstead, John Keats puts it:

“Life is but a day, a fragile dew drop on its perilous way from a tree’s summit”, - we need to savor every moment of it.

Which is why at Hampstead Bakery and Cafe, we’re bringing back the vibe of a rural area, rich with greenery and beautiful scenery for you to take a brief escape from your urban life for a heartbeat, & reboot your sensory with our selection of baked goodness

THE BAKERY

We are offering a journey to the rural areas in London through our selection of freshly made breads and sandwiches. For the sweet escape, we have a range of biscuits, desserts and the world-famous knickerbocker Sundae
